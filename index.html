Day 1

Day 1: ensuring the safety of the cetizens in Sitio Bebe, Community cleaning would be done. This is being done to improve mental health, to prevent sickness, and to encourage collaborations. And the expected result of this activity is to have a cleaner and safer community and to improve collaboration effort.

Day 2

Day 2: We will provide some medicines or first aid kits.We will be setting up medical tents where the citizens of Sitio Bebe can get free check ups from nurses who works at a small clinic and are willing to help those who are in need.

x

Day 3

Day 3: For day 3 since we will be using the money provided, we will use it to buy groceries for the people including canned goods, hygiene kits, rice, noodles and more. After assembling all the essentials it will be handed out at the center of the community where everyone will line up for the goods. We would also focus on the ones who are struggling with economic challenges.
